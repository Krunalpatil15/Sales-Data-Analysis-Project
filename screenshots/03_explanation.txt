Analysis: Category Sales Analysis

Tool Used: Microsoft Excel Pivot Table + Pie Chart

Steps Performed:
1. Inserted a Pivot Table from the sales dataset containing 50,000 rows.
2. Dragged Category into Rows.
3. Dragged Sales into Values (Sum of Sales).
4. Created a Pie Chart from the Pivot Table for visual representation.

Result:
The Pivot Table and Pie Chart show total sales generated by each product category.
The chart highlights the percentage contribution of Electronics vs Accessories.

Insight:
- Electronics generate the highest revenue among all categories.
- Accessories have a lower but significant share of sales.
- Understanding category performance helps in inventory planning and marketing strategies.

Business Use:
- Helps management identify high-performing product categories.
- Guides marketing, promotions, and resource allocation based on category performance.
