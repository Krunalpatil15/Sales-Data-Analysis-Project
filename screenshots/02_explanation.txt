Analysis: Top Selling Products

Tool Used: Microsoft Excel Pivot Table + Pivot Chart

Steps Performed:
1. Inserted a Pivot Table from the sales dataset containing 50,000 rows.
2. Dragged Product into Rows.
3. Dragged Sales into Values (Sum of Sales).
4. Sorted products by total sales in descending order.
5. Created a Pivot Chart (Clustered Column) from the Pivot Table for visual representation.

Result:
The Pivot Table and Pivot Chart show total revenue generated by each product.
The chart clearly highlights which products are generating the highest sales.

Insight:
- Laptops and Phones are among the top-selling products.
- Monitors, Printers, and Accessories contribute moderate revenue.
- Headphones are also high-demand, indicating strong customer interest.

Business Use:
- Helps management focus on top-selling products for inventory and marketing.
- Provides guidance for promotions and stocking high-demand items.
