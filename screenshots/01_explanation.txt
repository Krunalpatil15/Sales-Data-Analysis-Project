Analysis: Total Sales by Region

Tool Used: Microsoft Excel Pivot Table + Pivot Chart

Steps Performed:
1. Opened the sales dataset containing 50,000 rows.
2. Inserted a Pivot Table from the dataset.
3. Dragged Region into Rows.
4. Dragged Sales into Values (Sum of Sales).
5. Created a Pivot Chart (Clustered Column) from the Pivot Table for visual representation.

Result:
The Pivot Table and Pivot Chart show total revenue generated in each region.
The chart provides a clear visual comparison of sales performance across regions.

Insight:
- North region generated the highest total sales.
- South, East, and West regions follow closely.
- This helps understand regional performance and revenue distribution.

Business Use:
- Identifies regions with the strongest and weakest sales performance.
- Helps management make data-driven decisions about marketing, resource allocation, and inventory management.
